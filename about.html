Our Story

Built by Players,
for Players.

PTG Activewear started on a training pitch in New Zealand and grew into a brand that believes every footballer deserves pro-level gear.

Our Mission

Performance Without Compromise

We founded PTG Activewear because we were tired of choosing between gear that performs and gear that looks good. Too many brands made you pick one. We decided to build something different.

Every PTG product is obsessively tested on real pitches by real players before it ever reaches you. If it doesn't survive a pre-season of hard training sessions, it doesn't get made.

We're a small team based in New Zealand, but we ship to players across the country — and we're proud of every order that goes out the door.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Performance First

Every design decision starts with one question: does this make the player better? Style follows function — always.

Uncompromising Quality

We use pro-grade fabrics and manufacturing. If it's not something we'd wear ourselves in a match, it doesn't leave our hands.

Community Driven

We listen to our players. New product ideas, sizing feedback, and design tweaks all come directly from the PTG community.

Proudly Kiwi

Designed in New Zealand for New Zealand conditions — and the growing global community of Kiwi footballers.

Fair Pricing

Pro-level quality shouldn't cost a fortune. We cut out the middlemen and sell direct so players get the best value possible.

Passion for the Game

We're footballers first, a brand second. That passion shows in every product we put our name on.
